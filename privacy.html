Privacy Policy

UBLpeek processes all invoice data locally in your browser. This 100% FREE extension does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal or business data.

  • Invoice processing: All UBL/XML parsing, validation, and PDF generation occurs client-side. Your invoice data never leaves your device.
  • Permissions used:
    • storage — saves user preferences (display settings, theme) and anonymous usage statistics locally.
    • activeTab — detects when XML/UBL files are open in the current browser tab.
  • Data we collect: Anonymous usage counts (invoices processed, features used) stored locally for 30 days, then automatically deleted.
  • Data we DON'T collect: Invoice content, personal information, browsing history, financial data, or account information.
  • Analytics/ads: None. No tracking, no external analytics, no advertisements.
  • Monetization: UBLpeek is completely free with no premium features, subscriptions, or payment processing.
